# Centrifugal-Pump-Project
This project focuses on the design and performance evaluation of a centrifugal pump system intended for efficient fluid transfer in industrial applications. ..
The system was tested under varying flow and head conditions to determine
its operational characteristics and overall efficiency. It achieves this by spinning
an impeller that forces fluid from the inlet towards the outlet, creating
a pressure differential that moves the fluid.
The pump achieved a maximum head of 1.95 meters and a peak flow
rate of 1.24 liters per minute. Electrical measurements during testing
indicated a typical operating voltage of 12 V and a current draw of
0.29 A. Performance analysis revealed a peak efficiency of 4.9%, which
occurred at a head of 1.76 meters.
Overall, these results indicate that the centrifugal pump operates effectively
within its intended design specifications, providing a reliable and
energy-efficient solution for applications requiring medium to high pumping
capacity.
